Cake Shoop

What is Cake Shoop?

Cakeshoop is a location in Girona/Salt, inaugurated in 2016, and currently one of the most famous pastry shops in the province of Catalonia, Spain. It is well-known for its high quality and innovative sweets.

The name cakeshoop comes from the City of Palms: It was an important city in Syria and was the capital of the so-called Palmyrene Empire before BC, ruled by Queen Zenobia.

Our products

1Our Best Handmade Product

Baklava: The best pastry we have at cakeshoop is baklava, which is made with layers of phyllo dough filled with nuts such as pistachios, walnuts, almonds, and any other nuts you like. This pastry, after a hard day's work, leaves you with a delicious taste in your mouth.

2Another Deslicious Pastry We Offer

Kunafeh: It's a nest-shaped dessert made of shredded phyllo dough that we call "Nightingale's Nest" because of its resemblance to a bird's nest, and we are very familiar with the nightingale bird in our country.
